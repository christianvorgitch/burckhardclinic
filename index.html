The Burckhard Clinic treats persons with the following mental health problems:

  • Chronic Depression
  • Acute Depression
  • Generalized Anxiety Syndrome
  • Panic Disorder
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorder
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder

People who may benefit from this type of therapy:

  • You have already tried counseling and are now ready for more intensive
  • You find that medication alone is not enough to manage your depression or anxiety
  • You find you are only surviving in life rather than thriving
  • You make poor life decisions when you are capable of better choices
  • You find self help books are not enough

Benefits of successful treatment include:

  • Improved mood
  • Decreased anxiety
  • Increased capacity to manage stress
  • Healthier self esteem
  • More satisfying interaction with others
  • Better conflict resolution
